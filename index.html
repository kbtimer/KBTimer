KBTimer

KBTimer is a simple web application for timing questions for Colorado Knowledge Bowl.

Open Now

Features and instructions (including phone and tablet installation):

  • The timer starts at 15 seconds.
  • Tap the screen to start the countdown.
  • Tap again to reset to 15 seconds.
  • Works even without an internet connection.
  • Can provide a spoken 5-second warning and call "time" when time is up (which is useful for running practice, but should probably not be used during tournaments).
  • The lower left has a continually counting clock as a backup for manual counting of time.
  • Tap the button in the lower right to jump to 5 seconds remaining.
  • Tap the button in the upper left to access the settings
  • Available settings:
    • Whether or not to speak the five second warning and time expiration
    • Which voice to use (available voices are device dependent)
    • How fast to speak
    • How many seconds to provide in the countdown (default 15)
  • Installation instructions:
    • Can be installed as an icon on the home screen of a phone or tablet. (Make sure to set your screen auto-lock delay to be long enough so that you don't end up with a black screen in the middle of practice or a round!)
    • Android: Open in Chrome. Tap the three dots in the upper right of the browser and select "Add to Home screen."
    • iOS: Open in Safari. In the browser controls, tap the icon that's a square with an arrow coming up out of it. Scroll over and select "Add to Home Screen."
